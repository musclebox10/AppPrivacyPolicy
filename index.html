Important: The Muscle Box App is intended for individual/personal use only. It does not represent or provide services on behalf of any gym, trainer, or organization.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, and profile details you provide during signup.
  • Fitness Data: Workout logs, BMI, body measurements, progress tracking, optional diet-related inputs.
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, IP address, and Firebase push token for notifications.
  • Usage Data: App activity such as screens visited, time spent, and features used.
  • Photos: Only accessed when you voluntarily upload images for challenges (e.g. 90-Day Selfie Challenge).
  • Rewards & Challenges: Data related to in-app challenges, reward points, and referral codes.

The Application does not collect or store precise real-time location data.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To track fitness progress and workout history.
  • To provide personalized motivational features such as in-app challenges and rewards.
  • To send reminders, updates, and push notifications.
  • To improve user experience and analyze app usage (anonymized).
  • To maintain secure login and protect user accounts.

3. Permissions We Request

  • Camera: For users who choose to take progress photos.
  • Photo Library: To upload challenge or profile pictures. The app does not access the entire gallery.
  • Internet Access: To sync your data securely with Firebase (authentication, storage, notifications).

4. Third-Party Access

We only share limited, anonymized data with the following third-party service providers to improve functionality and analytics:

  • Firebase Authentication
  • Firebase Firestore
  • Google Analytics for Firebase
  • Firebase Crashlytics

We may also share information:

  • To comply with legal obligations or court orders.
  • To investigate potential violations or fraud.
  • With service providers who help operate our services under strict confidentiality agreements.

We retain user data as long as you use the Application and for a reasonable period thereafter. If you wish to delete your account and all associated data, you can contact us at muscleboxin@gmail.com.

6. Security

We take your data security seriously. We implement physical, technical, and administrative measures to protect the information we collect and store. All data is securely stored using Firebase services with encryption and access controls.

7. Age Restrictions

This Application is intended for use only by individuals who are 18 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under the age of 18. If you are under 18, you may not use this Application or provide any personal information.

If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected personal information from anyone under the age of 18, we will take immediate steps to delete such data. If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at sarang.kukde@gmail.com so we can remove the information.
